## [1.0.0] - December 2025

### Release Highlights
First public release of the SentinelAI Compliance Firewall marketing website.

### Features
- **Homepage**: Landing page with hero section, bias categories overview, and interactive demo preview
- **About Page**: Company mission, team profiles with LinkedIn links
- **Product Page**: Feature overview and capabilities
- **Pricing Page**: Plan comparison with "Coming Soon" pricing and waitlist CTA
- **Security Page**: Security practices and compliance roadmap
- **API Documentation**: Planned API endpoints and integration examples with "Coming Soon" notice
- **Help Center**: FAQ, help categories, and contact options
- **Blog**: Coming soon with preview content
- **Contact**: Contact form for inquiries

### Waitlist System
- Email and name collection with Google Sheets integration
- Source tracking for marketing attribution
- Optional company name field

### Legal Pages
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Cookie Policy
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

### Coming Soon Pages
- Case Studies (accepting early partners)
- Community (launching soon)
- Status Page (real-time monitoring coming)
- API Access (early access waitlist available)
